Key Skills

Applied expertise across analog, RF, biomedical, FPGA and quantum systems

Analog Circuit Design: Strong expertise in designing and analyzing analog circuits for high-performance electronic systems.

Microwave Monolithic Integrated Circuits (MMICs): Specialized in analysis and design of reconfigurable power amplifiers for next-generation high-frequency wireless systems.

RF Integrated Circuits (RFICs): Proficient in CMOS and GaN MMIC-based power amplifier design, emphasizing efficiency, linearity, and reliability for embedded and high-speed applications.

Biomedical Applications: Experienced in developing carbon nanotube and graphene-based amplifiers, as well as specialized RF power amplifiers for medical diagnostic systems.

FPGA-Based Wireless Transceivers: Integrating RF front-ends with digital baseband systems on FPGA platforms for real-time wireless communication applications.

RF Power Amplifier Design & Modeling: Expertise in embedding device models into RF amplifier design to enhance performance prediction, characterization, and optimization.

Quantum Circuit Design: Knowledgeable in the principles and design methodologies of quantum circuits for emerging computing and communication technologies.
